# SLO Scorecard

## Purpose

Track how mature the SLO practice is for a given service portfolio.

## Score Areas

- critical service coverage
- SLI quality
- SLO realism
- review cadence
- response alignment

## Example Interpretation

- low score means the team lacks measurable reliability guidance
- mid score means SLOs exist but need consistency
- high score means SLOs are operating as a management tool
